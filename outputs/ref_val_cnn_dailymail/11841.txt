referee tabb has decided to leave the world snooker circuit
tabb ( left ) stands alongside ronnie o'sullivan during the 2012 world championship final tabb was the first female to referee a world championship final at the crucible theatre in 2009 she had previously played on the eight - ball pool circuit , and also captained the scottish ladies ' pool team
a world snooker statement read : ' tabb , one of snooker 's most experienced referees who took charge of two world championship finals , has left the world snooker circuit
' world snooker would like to take the opportunity to thank tabb for her contribution to world snooker over the last 14 years and wish her all the best with her future refereeing endeavours
