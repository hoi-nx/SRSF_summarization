" captain america " actor chris evans met some " true superheroes " on saturday at seattle children 's hospital in a visit with " guardians of the galaxy " star chris pratt

if chris evans ' team -- the patriots -- won , chris pratt would visit christopher 's haven in boston dressed as " guardians of the galaxy " character star-lord , wearing patriots quarterback tom brady 's jersey
chris pratt wore his star-lord costume to visit boston children 's hospital in february , and chris evans donned his captain america duds to visit seattle children 's hospital on saturday , bearing gifts in the form of marvel action figures and accessories
