the german grand prix has been dropped from this year 's formula one calendar , motor sport 's world governing body the fia has announced
home favourite nico rosberg won the german grand prix for mercedes last year officials from both circuits had voiced concern that time was running out to organise the race , which was scheduled to take place over the weekend of july 17 - 19
the decision means the 2015 season will be shortened to 19 races , with a three - week break between the british grand prix on july 5 and the hungarian grand prix on july 26
hockenheim and nurburgring have hosted the german grand prix for the past seven years the german grand prix has alternated between hockenheim and nurburgring since 2007
