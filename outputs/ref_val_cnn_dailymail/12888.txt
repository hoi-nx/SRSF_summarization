police in detroit made a gruesome discovery tuesday
a bailiff performing an eviction on a home on the east side of detroit called 911 after discovering the body of a female child inside a freezer
while investigating the body , authorities found a second body , a male , according to a release from the detroit police department
both bodies were inside a plastic bag , detroit police chief craig said
the mother of the children was apprehended at a separate location , and was later arrested on child abuse charges , pending the " results of the medical examiner 's report , " a release from the police department said
the names of the children have also not been released , but craig said the boy was approximately 11 years old and the girl was approximately 14
