the increase underscores china 's intention to prioritize military spending even as economic growth slows

china 's defense budget is still dwarfed by what the u.s. spends on its military -- $ 598 billion , according to 2014 figures provided by ihs
but , while china 's budget has increased by double digits every year since 2010 , u.s. spending has declined since then
