former thai prime minister yingluck shinawatra has been ordered to stand trial over her role in a controversial rice subsidy scheme that cost the country billions
the first court hearing will take place on may 19 , a statement from thai 's supreme court said
on her facebook page , yingluck shinawatra said she behaved with integrity and honesty during her time as prime minister
in january , thai 's military - appointed national legislative assembly ( nla ) voted in favor of impeaching yingluck shinawatra for her role in the rice program
