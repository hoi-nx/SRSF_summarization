new york cardinal edward egan , who led the diocese of new york for nearly a decade , died thursday at 82 , the archdiocese announced
edward egan was pronounced dead of a heart attack at nyu langone medical center at 2:20 p.m. " thank god he had a peaceful death , passing away right after lunch today , " cardinal timothy dolan , edward egan 's successor and current archbishop of new york , said in a statement
edward egan was appointed archbishop of new york in 2000 and later cardinal in 2001 by pope john paul ii. egan retired from the archdiocese in 2009
as the leader of one of the largest catholic communities in the country , edward egan oversaw a growth of more than 200,000 registered parishioners and welcomed pope benedict xvi on a visit to new york in 2008 to commemorate the 200th anniversary of the founding of the diocese of new york
" edward egan spread love and knowledge , and brought comfort to countless new york and others across the country and the world who sought his guidance and counsel -- especially in the aftermath of 9/11 , " said new york mayor bill de blasio in a statement
