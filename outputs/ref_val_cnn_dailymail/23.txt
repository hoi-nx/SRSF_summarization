the nigeria presidential elections has been won by the opposition party for the first time in the country 's history
general buhari beat incumbent president goodluck jonathan by 2.1million votes in a historic win which has seen thousands take to the streets in celebration
goodluck jonathan conceded this afternoon and called 72 - year - old general buhari to congratulate him , aviation minister osita chidoka said
after the votes from the country 's 36 states and small federal capital territory were in this evening , buhari was found to have won with 15.4 million votes against goodluck jonathan 's 13.3 million
general buhari , who has previously made three failed attempts to win the presidency , ruled nigeria from 1984 until mid- 1985 , after a seizing power in a military coup
buhari was eventually was overthrown by his own soldiers after attempting to cover up nigeria 's deepening economic crisis , by silencing the press and jailing journalists
