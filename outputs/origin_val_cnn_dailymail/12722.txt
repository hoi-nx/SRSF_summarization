three french sports stars were among 10 people killed when two helicopters collided in argentinians on monday evening , authorities said
the helicopters were reported to be heading to a gorge in northwestern argentinians for the filming of the reality tv show " dropped " for the french broadcaster tf1
eight french passengers and the choppers ' two argentinians pilots died in the midair crash near the town of villa castelli in la rioja province , argentinians 's state news agency telam reported , citing local authorities
the dead included the famed sailor florence arthaud , who in 1990 broke the record for crossing the north atlantic alone ; the swimmer camille muffat , who won three medals at the 2012 london olympics ; and the boxer alexis vastine , who won a bronze medal at the 2008 olympics in beijing
