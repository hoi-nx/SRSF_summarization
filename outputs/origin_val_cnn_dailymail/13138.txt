the first body was found thursday morning by the woman 's companion , a 40 - year - old man , in a freezer bag , bfmtv reported
police discovered bodies of four other babies in a freezer that evening during a search of the family 's home , the channel said
officials with the bordeaux prosecutor 's office were scheduled to hold a press conference friday afternoon

the woman 's companion was taken into custody , the reports said

the couple is reported to have two daughters , aged 13 and 15 , and to have led a seemingly routine life
