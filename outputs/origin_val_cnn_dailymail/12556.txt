moscow russian president putin awarded medals monday to chechen leader ramzan kadyrov and to andrei lugovoi , the suspect in the murder of former russian security agent alexander litvinenko , among more than 30 other honorees
ramzan kadyrov was given the order of merit
the decree signed by putin states : " for work achievements , active social activities and many years of diligent work the order of merit is awarded to ramzan kadyrov , the president of the chechen
" andrei lugovoi , the suspect in the poisoning death of former russian security agent alexander litvinenko , was given a second - class medal of the order of merit for the motherland , according to putin 's decree
sergey kislyak , a diplomat who 's been serving as russian 's ambassador to the u.s. since 2008 , was also among the honorees
he was cited as an " honored employee of a diplomatic service
" andrei lugovoi is an mp in russian 's lower house of parliament for the nationalist and pro-kremlin liberal democratic party
he is deputy chairman of the lower house 's security and anti-corruption committee
he also has hosted a show called " traitors " on russian tv and runs a restaurant in moscow
ramzan kadyrov has spoken out about the assassination of opposition leader nemtsov , defending zaur dadayev , one of the chechen charged in the shooting
" zaur dadayev was a russian interior ministry officer who served with distinction , " ramzan kadyrov said on his instagram account
putin 's spokesman dmitry peskov said it was a coincidence that the arrest of suspects in nemtsov 's killing happened around the same time as the award to the chechen president
in an interview with russian business outlet rbc , he stressed that the decree on awards was filed several months ago .
