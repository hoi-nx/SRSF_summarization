the football association will not be allowed to lay a wreath in turin this week to mark the 30th anniversary of the heysel disaster
with england playing italy at the juventus stadium on tuesday , the football association wanted to take the opportunity to commemorate the deaths of 39 supporters before the 1985 european cup final between liverpool and juventus by laying a wreath at a memorial outside the ground in turin
the italian club said they appreciated the gesture , but rejected the offer , claiming they did not want to detract from their own memorial in may
the football association said it respected juventus ' wishes
