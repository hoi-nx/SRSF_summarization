kathmandu , nepal the clamor and chaos of the previous day has dissipated by the time we arrive at kathmandu 's only airport
the mad rush of 24 hours previously , in those first confusing , cacophonous hours following the 7.8 - magnitude earthquake near the nepal capital , had died down
it is an overwhelming introduction to this city that , less than 48 hours ago , was hit by the worst earthquake this country has experienced in 80 years
taking into account the fact that many rural areas , just as badly affected but isolated and vulnerable , have yet to be evaluated , the human cost is staggering
across town the bus station is a hive of activity as scores try urgently to leave the city , to make it out to the outlying areas so badly affected by this quake
the scene is repeated at every gas station ; snaking lines of indian - made tata cars , and motorcycles , waiting to fill up
remaining inside kathmandu , neighbors stare forlornly at their former homes , now collapsed piles of rubble
we visit a montessori school , mercifully empty as the children had the saturday off

the pastor 's son tamang clambers up a ladder , looking for an entrance into the ruined facade , looking to retrieve his father , not knowing if he will find him alive or dead

a nearby five - story structure has collapsed in on itself
