china police have arrested more than 133,000 people and seized 43.3 tons of illegal narcotics during a six - month anti-drug campaign , the country 's ministry of public security has announced
authorities also handled 115,000 drug - related crimes -- such as robbery -- and 606,000 cases of drug use during the nationwide campaign to " ban drugs in hundreds of cities , " liu , assistant minister of public security , said wednesday
liu said china 's drug - related problems were still severe , with online drug trafficking an increasing problem
he said the ministry had launched a three - month online campaign starting in april targeting people engaged in drug - related internet crimes
