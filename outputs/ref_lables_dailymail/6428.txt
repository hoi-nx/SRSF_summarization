the newspaper won three pulitzer prizes for international reporting and feature photography for its coverage of the west africa ebola outbreak
the pulitzer committee at columbia university in new york city said new york times staff shared the prize for international reporting for its coverage of the deadly ebola epidemic in west africa
new york times reporter eric lipton took the prize for investigative reporting on the influence of lobbyists
and freelance photographer for the paper , daniel berehulak , won the feature photography award for his ebola coverage
